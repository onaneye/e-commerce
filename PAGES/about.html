Who Are We?

PnS is a Luxury Party Rental Company located in the heart of Lagos, serving the Etti Osa areas and its environs.  It’s repertoire of available items diversified from a mere 30 tables and 300 chairs to include exciting array of exquisite linen, fine china, cutlery, stemware, and elegant décor. Under the guidance of our qualified staff, our customers are treated to the utmost in party and banquet essentials. We pride ourselves on being reliable to each customer and strive for excellence in our mission to assist with your event needs. When you choose PnS, your event becomes ours and our team of experts will assist you in assembling all the supplies you will need to put on a spectacular and a memorable event. PnS is the latest source for all your party rental needs. Whether you are planning a luxury corporate event, graduation party, backyard party, or an elegant wedding and reception banquet, PnS can create an amazing setting to make your event special. We are fully equipped to handle any event; big or small, from tables and chairs to beautiful décor with exceptional quality. Envelop your guests with surreal of party experience with touch of elegance and set the stage for a distinctive event.
